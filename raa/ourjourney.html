Our Journey towards package-free

RAA began with 3 students and a shared passion to combat climage change

We've introduced a competitively priced and community friendly package-free store to encourage sustainable and eco-friendly shopping.

As biology students confronted with the challenges of climate change, we experienced the environmental effects of modern throw-away society. Seeing no quick-fix to waste management, we realised change needs to happen from the inside out.

As part of the solution, Raa empowers Danes with a simple approach towards sustainable and eco-friendly shopping habits.

What or who initiated your love for the environment and its sustainability?

Ever since the first announcements of the global warming i’ve been doing small things as turning off the lights because i REALLY love nature and can’t bear the thought of destroying it. So last year when, I started studying Biology and participated in a beach clean up at our west coast it got clear to me how serious the problem is, and ever since then, i’ve done my best to change habits, and live more sustainable.

What is your favourite bulk good we can find in your store?

I think my favourite bulk must be the Hampefrø. I love that they are so healthy, local and very easy to produce. In my opinion they must be the most sustainable alternative to all the superfoods like chiaseads we import.

What or who initiated your love for the environment and its sustainability?
