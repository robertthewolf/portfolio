Top 5 Web Design Trends for Autumn 2017

The design world is constantly evolving and sometimes it's hard follow what's new. To make it easier for you, here are the most popular design trends for this autumn!

Open Composition

With modern technologies such as css3, layout on the web has now no limits!

Cinemagraphs

More than just a picture! There's something magical about cinemagraphs, it's a bit of a mind-trick. Although they have been around for some time, their potential for the web is still undiscovered.

Human's brain loves motion, so cinemagraphs are a great to emphasize on a certain part of your website.

Are you looking for some free cinemagraphs to use in your own project? Try looking at GalleReplay or Brust.

Big Typography

Go big or go home! Using beatuful typefaces allows webdesigners to express themselves like never before, and what's more, bigger font size contrast improves visual hierarchy and simple makes the design look sharper!

Gradients

Gradients are back! With a careful choice of colors, mostly monochromatic or analogic, gradients adds fresh and modern look.

Custom Illustrations

Vector graphics is the future! Illustrations are a great way how to make your website outstand. In a certain way, illustrations are a form of art, and honestly, who doesn't love art? Browsing the web can be as enjoyable as a walk in an art gallery!
