Godsbanen

The freeway is located in the center of Aarhus and forms a framework for meetings between arts, business and education, and it is open to everyone every day, whether you just want to look around or whether you want to take a living with the creative art forms. The house offers both theater, open scenes, restaurant, art exhibitions, conference facilities, venue, workshops, art production facilities, literature readings, special class architecture and much more.
