Institut for (X) is a culture and business platform for young designers, musicians, artists, entrepreneurs and craftsmen located in Aarhus K.

It’s an independent and not-for-profit culture association arising from citizen initiatives.

There are currently around 90 projects, 50 businesses and 35 associations.

Do you have an idea you would like to implement in Aarhus K? Institut for (X) coordinates the creative urban experiments which take place here.

From viking martial arts practitioners to designers, jazz musicians to beer brewers - your interest can help to make Aarhus a better city.

If you have a project you feel will make Aarhus K a vibrant, exciting and fun place to be, come on over.

We'd love to hear from you!
