What is Aarhus K?

Aarhus K is a district of Aarhus dedicated to urban experiments.

A place where the community collectively curates the development of the area.

In 2009, Institut for (X) began transforming the former railway area into an organic city.

The vision is to facilitate and enhance cultural activities, combining artistic creativity with business, public debate and public education.

The mission is the best possible neighborhood for all. Your ideas are welcome here!
